Senior Thesis Results and Discussion

Results

We collected data from 253 subjects overall, including those who were excluded for failing the headphone check.

Headphone Check Data

64 particpiants failed more than one of the headphone check trials and were excluded from completing the rest of the experiment, leaving 189 participants who continued and provided responses. This is a 0.25 subject failure rate.

Taking a closer look at the distribution of trials passed by subject, we see that, overall, 0.79 of the trails were passed. The mean number of trials passed per subject was 4.76; this number can be differentiated into 5.86 for those who passed the check (with no more than one trial failure) and 1.52 for those who did not pass the check.
